About PharmaGuard

An AI-powered pharmacogenomic risk prediction system built for RIFT 2026 Hackathon.

Why Pharmacogenomics?

Every patient's DNA is unique. Genetic variants in drug-metabolizing genes like CYP2D6 and TPMT determine how a patient processes medication ‚Äî too fast, too slow, or not at all.

PharmaGuard analyzes these variants from standard VCF files and provides instant, clinically validated risk predictions aligned with CPIC guidelines.

Our AI generates explainable recommendations that cite specific genetic variants ‚Äî giving clinicians the context they need to make informed prescribing decisions.
